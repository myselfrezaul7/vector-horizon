Study in Australia

Sun, surf, and surprisingly excellent universities. Australia's got the perfect combo of laid-back vibes and serious academics.

Let's Talk Why Australia?

The Australia Advantage

It's not just the kangaroos (though they're pretty cool too).

Globally Respected Unis

7 Australian universities sit in the world's top 100. Employers know an Aussie degree means business.

Work While You Learn

You can work 48 hours per fortnight during semester and full-time during breaks. Enough to cover your expenses (and maybe some beach trips).

The Lifestyle, Though

Beaches that look photoshopped, weather that spoils you, and a culture that knows how to have fun. Studies and adventures aren't mutually exclusive here.

Stick Around After

The post-study work visa gives you 2-4 years to build your career in Australia. Plenty of time to figure out your next move.
